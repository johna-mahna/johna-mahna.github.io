Meaty project

City Guides; Solely led design for a new travel product.

The Times is already a known authority on travel, with well-established, recognizable franchises such as 52 Places and 36 Hours. City Guides hopes to find a balance between the inspiration of 52 Places and the on-the-ground itinerary of 36 Hours. It provides a practical yet comprehensive look at a city, guiding readers towards specific travel coverage for their needs.
